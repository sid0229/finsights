Introduction to Personal Finance

Personal finance is the process of planning and managing your financial activities, including income generation, spending, saving, investing, and protection.

  • 8. Get Insurance: Protect yourself with health, life, and property insurance.
  • 9. Increase Financial Knowledge: Educate yourself on financial strategies and market trends.
  • 10. Review and Adjust Your Plan: Regularly assess your financial plan to align with your goals.
